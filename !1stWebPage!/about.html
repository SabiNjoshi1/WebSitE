COMPANY INFORMATION

It is our great pleasure to introduce Trek with Sabin Pvt. Ltd. is a reputed and responsible local trekking and tour agency, situated in Kathmandu Valley, Nepal. After decades of long experience in trekking and climbing field, we decided to establish our own trekking company and implement the knowledge through our own investments.

About Us

We are a Nepal’s Government registered local trekking agency with the register number: 00005/072/073 having decades of experience in Nepal trekking and expeditions.
 
We acquire qualified and professional staff, who actively serving the trekking and tour business for many years. The safety, security, comfort, and satisfaction of our clients have been our main priority for all these years. We are the firmest name in trekking, mountaineering, tour, mountain flight, rafting, and other outdoor adventures sports activities.
 
We are well known not only for providing a high standard of service for the Trekking groups because our concern is also the realization of social fairness and ecological responsibility. We not only know the importance of preserving our cultural and natural heritage. We indeed are cleaning campgrounds behind us already for many years-according to our slogan: leave only nothing behind except joy.
 

Mission Statement:

  • To provide high-quality service to our clients and satisfy their needs for a happy holiday in Nepal.
  • To demonstrate our Company’s sense of responsibility with respect to the social issues of the local people, as well as all aspects of good ecological practices.
